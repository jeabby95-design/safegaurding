Signs to Watch For

Recognizing the signs of abuse or neglect is crucial for effective safeguarding. Some common indicators include:

  • Unexplained injuries or frequent bruises
  • Changes in behavior or mood
  • Withdrawal from social interactions
  • Poor hygiene or malnutrition
  • Fearfulness around certain individuals

Action Steps

If you suspect someone is at risk, take the following steps:

  • Report your concerns to the appropriate authorities or safeguarding team.
  • Document any observations or conversations that raise concern.
  • Offer support to the individual, ensuring their safety and well-being.
  • Follow up to ensure that appropriate actions have been taken.
  • Do not promise confidentiality.
  • Call 999 if an emergency exists.
